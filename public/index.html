1.1 PRIVACY POLICY

February 09, 2017 This page details our privacy policy regarding Tap Manager. When playing our game, no information is collected from you apart from anonymous usage statistics (using Unity Analytics).

1.1.1 WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Tap Manager itself does not collect any personally identifiable information from anybody. Some games may collect anonymous usage statistics & analytics, such as matches won etc. These analytics are used to improve our games, such as fine-tuning the gameplay experience.

1.1.2 WHAT INFORMATION DO OUR TRUSTED BUSINESS PARTNERS COLLECT?

As our game is primarily ad supported, non-personally identifiable information (for example your device model, or your country/continent level location) may be collected by our trusted business partners so that they can serve the ads you see. Also, for social gaming features (such as global leaderboards, achievements and score sharing on social networks) our trusted partners may collect information they need to provide the services they offer. This information is non-personally identifiable except for the cases where you explicitly register (or login) with your e-mail address, for example. Our game will not collect personal information without your consent. Please check the list below, if you wish to have further information about our partners. For Tap Manager, we are currently working with (or utilize the services provided by) following partners: • For analytics: Unity Analytics (privacy policy) • For ads: Unity Ads (privacy policy) Apart from the trusted partners listed above, no information is disclosed to third parties.
